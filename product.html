About us

At We Network LLC, we specialize in delivering cutting-edge telecommunications infrastructure solutions designed to meet the demands of a rapidly evolving digital landscape. Our expertise spans key areas, including Telecom Infrastructure, Radio Network Optimization Planning (RNO-RNT), and Microwave Rollouts, enabling us to provide comprehensive services that enhance connectivity and operational efficiency.

We are committed to excellence in all aspects of our work, from meticulous operation and maintenance to ICT, 5G private networks and MEP solutions. At We Network LLC, we believe in building strong partnerships that empower businesses to thrive in an interconnected world.

EQUIPMENT SHELTER

Our equipment shelter is built with durable steel and anti-corrosion coatings to withstand harsh environments. It includes a thermal management system with efficient AC units to maintain optimal temperatures for telecom equipment. Backup power solutions, including UPS and advanced battery systems, ensure uninterrupted service. With secure access control, remote monitoring via IoT, and real-time diagnostics, it offers robust security and management. Its modular design allows for easy expansion and customization, making it ideal for telecom tower infrastructure.

STRUCTURAL TOWERS

WE NETWORK LLC is a leading provider of engineered structural towers, meeting strict industry standards. Our product range includes Roof Top Towers (RTT) from 4 to 9 meters, and Ground-Based Towers ranging from 12 to 100 meters. We use high-strength steel and advanced welding techniques to ensure durability and structural integrity. Our towers are designed to support various telecom and networking equipment with adaptable installation options for each project. We offer comprehensive solutions, including design, manufacturing, and supply.

GREEN FIELD STRUCTURES

WE NETWORK LLC provides a wide range of advanced green field structures, including tubular, triangular, and latticed towers in 3-legged and 4-legged designs, with heights from 25 to 125 meters. We offer complete site setups, including fencing, assembly, installation, and implementation of towers. Our towers come in various sizes, from 6m to 100m, designed to meet the toughest technical requirements, making us a leader in scalable infrastructure solutions.

MONOPOLES

Our monopole towers, available in heights of 25, 30, 35, 40, and 50 meters, feature custom design engineering. Key specifications include:

Operational Wind Speed: 120 km/h

Design Wind Speed: 140 km/h

Antenna Capacity: Supports up to 3 multi-band antennas, 12 RRU modules, 3 junction boxes, and 4 microwave antennas (0.6 meters in diameter).

These towers provide exceptional strength and reliability, ensuring seamless connectivity for your technical projects.

Camouflage Tower

At WE Network, we specialize in the deployment of camouflage towers—innovative telecommunications structures designed to seamlessly blend into their surroundings. Utilizing materials and designs that mimic natural features like trees or local architecture, our camouflage towers minimize visual disruption while enhancing community acceptance of essential infrastructure. This approach not only ensures reliable connectivity for our clients but also reflects our commitment to sustainable development, balancing the need for advanced communication networks with environmental considerations and the aesthetic integrity of residential and scenic areas.

Pole Antenna

We Network LLC also specialize in pole antennas, which are sleek, vertical structures designed to support wireless communication while maintaining a low visual profile. These antennas can be integrated into existing urban environments, such as streetlights, utility poles, or building facades, allowing for efficient coverage without the bulkiness of traditional towers. Our pole antennas provide robust connectivity solutions, ensuring reliable service for both urban and rural areas while minimizing visual impact. By focusing on innovative design and placement, WE Network enhances communication infrastructure in a way that respects the aesthetic character of communities, making connectivity both effective and unobtrusive.
