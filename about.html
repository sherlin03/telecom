About us

At We Network LLC, we specialize in delivering cutting-edge telecommunications infrastructure solutions designed to meet the demands of a rapidly evolving digital landscape. Our expertise spans key areas, including Telecom Infrastructure, Radio Network Optimization Planning (RNO-RNT), and Microwave Rollouts, enabling us to provide comprehensive services that enhance connectivity and operational efficiency.

We are committed to excellence in all aspects of our work, from meticulous operation and maintenance to ICT, 5G private networks and MEP solutions. Our experienced team leverages innovative strategies and the latest technologies to ensure that our clients benefit from reliable, high-performance networks tailored to their specific needs. At We Network LLC, we believe in building strong partnerships that empower businesses to thrive in an interconnected world.

We Network Legacy

Yogendra Katiyar, drawing upon his extensive experience in telecommunication tower construction gained in India and Oman, recognized the potential for innovation in this sector. With his technical acumen and visionary ideas, he embarked on a transformative journey, founding a new company( We Network LLC ) in Oman in 2011. Specializing in a diverse range of services including Enterprise IT Solutions, Oil & Gas services, Telecom Tower and Infrastructure Solutions, MEP Solutions (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing), and Managed Services, We Network LLC quickly emerged as a pioneering force in the industry. The success achieved with We Network spurred Yogendra to expand his services further, leading to the establishment of VYK in India and WE Communication LLC in Dubai respectively. Currently, he is spearheading initiatives to implement Artificial Intelligence and 5G technologies within his networking companies, aiming to make a profound impact on the world.
